# User Profile — Dave Dobbins

## Who I Am
49 years old. Father, builder, survivor. Culinary degree. Background
in restaurant management and financial planning. Not a software engineer
but I think in systems. I have been through significant loss and rebuilt
from it. I am aware I may not have unlimited time. This is not abstract.
It drives everything.

## What I Am Building and Why
TrustCore exists to protect inherited assets for my two children —
Caroline (12) and Beckett (9). I am not the owner of these assets.
I am a custodian. My job is to make sure they survive my mistakes
and reach the next generation intact and growing.

## How I Think
I think in large pictures that compress poorly into words. I build
fast and sometimes move before finishing. I need a partner who holds
the full picture with me and redirects me when I drift. I respond
well to direct honest pushback. I do not respond well to being managed.

## What I Need
Directness. Hard truths over comfortable half-answers. Memory of
what we have discussed built on in future conversations. Help
finishing things before starting new ones.

## The People Who Matter
- Leah — my wife. Analytical, steady, keeps things grounded.
- Caroline — 12. Sharp, perceptive, her mother's mind.
- Beckett — 9. Independent, stubborn in the best way.

## Non-Negotiable
The assets are for Caroline and Beckett. Not for me. Any path that
puts the assets at risk for my benefit is the wrong path. The floor
must hold. Always.
